## What is PowerDown™️

Zed Hosting is proud to present you with a new era of pricing and savings from a hosting provider.  Zed Hosting's PowerDown™️ Feature makes it so that you only need to pay for the RAM, CPU, and Storage while its in use and are allocated the resources to reuse when the game is powered off, you only pay for the storage.
